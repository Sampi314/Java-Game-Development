How to Play:

  1. Click on food buttons (Burger, Pizza, Coffee) in the kitchen to start cooking
  2. Wait for the progress bar to fill
  3. Click "Add to Tray" when food is ready
  4. Wait for customers to arrive at tables (they spawn every 5 seconds)
  5. Click "Serve" button on a table that has a customer
  6. Watch the waiter walk to the kitchen, pick up food, then walk to the table!

Troubleshooting:

If you can't serve food:
1. Make sure you've cooked food first (it appears in "Ready to Serve" section)
2. Wait for a customer to arrive at a table
3. The "Serve" button will only work if you have the food the customer wants
4. Open browser console (F12) to check for errors
